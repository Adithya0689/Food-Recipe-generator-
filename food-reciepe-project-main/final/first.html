Carne Asada

Start your day with a burst of flavor by savoring tender and mouthwatering Carne Asada. This top-rated Carne Asada recipe offers a delightful and effortless dining experience. You'll be able to enjoy this delicious dish in just a short time, making it a perfect choice for a quick and satisfying meal. Discover how to prepare, store, and serve the best Carne Asada for a taste of authentic Mexican cuisine right at home.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Marinate the Meat: Start by marinating your choice of beef. In a mixing bowl, combine ingredients like lime juice, orange juice, garlic, cumin, chili powder, and salt.

step 2

Grill the Meat: While grilling, you can baste the meat with any remaining marinade for extra flavor.

step 3

Rest and Slice: Once cooked, transfer the meat to a cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute and keeps the meat tender.

ingredients

2 pounds of thinly sliced beef. Skirt steak or flank steak are popular choices for Carne Asada.

1/4 cup freshly squeezed lime juice

1/4 cup freshly squeezed orange juice

Salt and black pepper, to taste

tools

Grill

Resealable Plastic Bag

Measuring Spoons

Whisk or Mixing Utensil

Measuring Cups
