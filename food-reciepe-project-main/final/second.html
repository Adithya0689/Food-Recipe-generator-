Greek Ribs

Start your day on a savory note with a plate of mouthwatering Greek ribs. This delectable dish is bursting with flavors that transport your taste buds straight to the Mediterranean. Our top-rated Greek ribs recipe is not only incredibly delicious but also surprisingly simple to prepare. You can have these succulent ribs ready in no time, making it a perfect choice for a delightful dinner. Discover how to create, store, and savor the best Greek ribs for a memorable dining experience.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Marinate the Ribs: In a bowl, combine Greek-inspired marinade ingredients such as olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, and salt. Coat the ribs thoroughly with this mixture and let them marinate for at least 30 minutes or longer for maximum flavor.

step 2

Grill or Bake: Preheat your grill or oven to the desired temperature. Grill the marinated ribs until they are tender and have a delicious char, or bake them in the oven until they are cooked through and slightly crispy on the outside. This typically takes around 30-45 minutes, depending on your chosen method and rib thickness.

step 3

Serve: Once cooked to perfection, remove the Greek ribs from the grill or oven. Garnish with fresh herbs like parsley and serve them with classic Greek sides such as tzatziki sauce, pita bread, and a Greek salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

ingredients

3-4 pounds of pork or beef ribs

1/4 cup olive oil, 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice

Salt and black pepper, to taste

tools

Grill or Oven

Aluminium Foil

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
