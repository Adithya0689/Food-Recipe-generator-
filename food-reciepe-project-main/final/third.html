Vegetable Soup

In just a short 30 minutes, you can enjoy a warm and satisfying bowl of goodness, making it a perfect choice for a quick and wholesome lunch or dinner. instructions

step 1

Prepare the Vegetables:Wash, peel (if necessary), and chop a variety of fresh vegetables such as carrots, celery, onions, and bell peppers. You can also add ingredients like potatoes, zucchini, or tomatoes for extra flavor and texture.

step 2

Saute the Aromatics: In a large pot, heat a bit of olive oil or butter over medium heat. Add finely chopped onions and garlic. Saut√© until they become fragrant and translucent, usually taking about 3-5 minutes.

step 3

Simmer with Broth:Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Let it simmer until the vegetables become tender, typically around 20-30 minutes.
